Our Facilities

At Baywash Southside we are proud of our diverse range of facilities and are constantly adding new & improved services to increase functionality for our customers

Self-Serve Wash Bays 

Our self-serve wash bays allow you to wash your own car quickly and affordably with an easy to use system that also accepts cash & card payments.



Automatic Wash Bays 

Our Automatic Wash Bays allow you to relax in the comfort of your car while our machines do the work around you! There are various options to choose from as explained on our ‘prices’ page. During staffed hours one of our friendly team members will be able to take your payment and assist you through the process. Tap & Go facilities coming soon to automatic wash bays!

Vacuum Bays

We have a dedicated vacuum bay area which allows you to give the interior of your vehicle the Baywash treatment also! This is a self-serve area and is easy to use with special fragrance options as well to ensure your vehicle not only looks clean but smells fresh!
