Frequently Asked Questions

Are we open 24/7 ?

Yes we are open 24/7 with both staffed and unstaffed hours. All of the facilities are still available outside of staffed hours.

When are we staffed ?

Staffed hours are daily between 8am-5pm.

Is EFTPOS available ?

Tap & Go Card services are currently available in our self-serve wash bays and are coming very soon to our Automatic Bays. During staffed hours a team member can process a card payment for our Automatic Bays.

What to do when I arrive ?

If you are visiting during staffed hours, follow the prompts around to the entry point of your desired bay and a staff member will be able to assist. Outside of staffed hours simply follow the prompts and process your payment through your desired bay before continuing onto the wash process. Our facilities are extremely user friendly.
